Rotel Tacos

Easy to make recipe, with available ingredients.

These Rotel tacos are salty, slightly spicy, creamy, and crunchy all at once.

Itâ€™s like your favorite dip meets the perfect bite of a taco.

I recommend extra lettuce for that perfect cold crunch against the rich filling

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 lb ground sirloin
  • 2 tablespoons low sodium Taco seasoning
  • 8 ounces of good cheese, cut into large chunks
  • 1 (10-ounce) can diced tomatoes with green chiles
  • 8 to 10 crunchy taco shells
  • shredded iceberg lettuce
  • toppings of choice such as diced tomato, hot sauce and sour cream (optional)
