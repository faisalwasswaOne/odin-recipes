Sour Cream and Potato Salad

Fresh potato salad

Easy to make recipe, with available ingredients.

This salad is incredible, creamy, and crunchy all at once.

Itâ€™s like your favorite dip meets the perfect bite of a potato.

I recommend extra sour cream for that perfect cold crunch against the rich filling

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 lb yukon gold potatoes, peeled
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 2 teaspoons honey
  • 2 teaspoons dijon mustard or stone ground mustard
  • 1 clove clove garlic, finely chopped or pressed
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/4 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/4 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1/8 medium red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon chopped chives
